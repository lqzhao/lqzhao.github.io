Linqing Zhao（赵林清）

I'm currently a Postdoctoral fellow of Department of Automation, Tsinghua University, affiliated with Intelligent Vision Group (IVG), supervised by Prof. Prof. Jiwen Lu.

I received the B.Eng. degree in electronic information engineering and the Ph.D. degree in information and communication engineering from the School of Electrical and Information Engineering, Tianjin University, China, in 2017 and 2024, respectively, supervised by Prof. Zhanjie Song. During my PhD studies, I was honored to be a visiting student at Intelligent Vision Group (IVG), supervised by Prof. Jie Zhou and Prof. Jiwen Lu.
My research interests lie in computer vision, especially robot vision, autonomous driving perception, and deep learning.
